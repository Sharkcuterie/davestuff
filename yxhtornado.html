A tornadic supercell near Medicine Hat, AB



What happened?

On July 18, 2022, a supercell thunderstorm moved through the area near Medicine Hat, AB. This thunderstorm caused some damage, and it was determined that the damage was from both tornadic winds and straight-line winds.
The RADAR imagery from this event is presented here.
